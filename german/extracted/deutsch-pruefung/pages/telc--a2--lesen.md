### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The official telc Deutsch A2 Lesen subtest is completed inside the shared 50-minute Lesen und Schreiben block. It has 15 items across three Teile and focuses on practical written texts from everyday life.

#### Teil 1: Aufgaben und Information (Tasks and Information)

• Read the situations and one information text

• Choose the correct answer: a, b, or c

#### Teil 2: Text und Aussagen (Text and Statements)

• Read one longer text

• Decide whether each statement is richtig (+) or falsch (-)

#### Teil 3: Anzeigen und Situationen (Advertisements and Situations)

• Read several advertisements and short situations

• Match the correct advertisement to each situation
