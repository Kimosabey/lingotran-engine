## 🧭 Quick answer (TL;DR)

TELC and Goethe-Zertifikat both align with CEFR standards and assess the same four skills — Lesen, Hören, Schreiben, and Sprechen. Goethe runs A1–C2 worldwide, while TELC offers general and specialist formats (including TELC Deutsch C1 Hochschule for university access).

### Planning university study?

Prefer TELC C1 Hochschule or Goethe C1. Always double check the exact certificate requested by your university or Studienkolleg.

### Visas & employment

For work, family reunion, or integration, book whichever exam offers a convenient date at the required level (A1/B1/B2) in your city.

### Preparation tip

Build a realistic Prüfungstraining routine: timed mock exams with audio, transcripts, and speaking partners beat passive study every time.

## 🏢 What are Goethe-Zertifikat and TELC?

### 🇩🇪 Goethe-Zertifikat

Delivered by the Goethe-Institut with centres on every continent. Exams cover A1 through C2 and are widely recognised for visas, professional licensing, and study programs.

### 🌐 TELC – The European Language Certificates

Offers general and specialist exams from A1 to C2, including TELC Deutsch C1 Hochschuleaimed at university admission requirements.

## 📈 Levels A1–B2 at a glance

### A1 · A2

Everyday basics — introductions, short notes, simple leisure or travel chats.

### B1

Independent day-to-day German — complaints, informal letters, sharing experiences.

### B2

Upper-intermediate — understand main ideas in complex texts and join professional discussions.

## 🧾 A1 exam format — what to expect

| Skill | What happens | Your focus |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Lesen (Reading) | 6–7 short texts — notices, emails, timetables. Choose the matching statement or picture. | Underline key info (dates, locations, verbs) before checking the options. |
| Hören (Listening) | Dialogues + announcements. Often two listens, except TELC phone tasks (one listen). | Preview answer choices so you know what information to catch the first time. |
| Schreiben (Writing) | Fill a short form and write a 30–50 word message (invitation, note, email). | Memorise greeting + ending templates; tick off every point in the prompt. |
| Sprechen (Speaking) | Introduce yourself, ask/answer simple questions, role-play a situational card. | Practise question words aloud (Wer? Was? Wann?). Keep answers short but complete. |

## 🌍 Where are the certificates accepted?

### Goethe-Zertifikat ✅

Recognised worldwide for visa applications, professional recognition, and numerous academic programs. Check the latest policy of your embassy or employer.

### TELC C1 Hochschule 🎓

Tailored to university admission. Many Hochschulen list it explicitly. Still, confirm acceptance with the admissions team before booking.

## 💻 Test delivery & logistics

### Goethe-Institut

- • Paper-based and digital formats (digital = laptops supplied on site).
- • Speaking remains face-to-face with one or two examiners.
- • Results typically arrive within 2–4 weeks.

### TELC

- • Predominantly paper-based; some levels available computer-based.
- • Speaking often completed with another candidate in a pair setting.
- • Result processing can take 4–6 weeks depending on the centre.

## 🤔 Which exam feels easier?

There is no universal “easier” exam. Both providers align with the same CEFR descriptors. Candidates who practise the exact task types (matching, note-taking, role-plays), manage exam timing, and speak confidently during the partner tasks tend to score higher — regardless of provider.

Tip: record yourself talking through the speaking prompts and compare your answers with native examples. This builds fluency and reduces exam-day anxiety.

## 📅 Prüfungstraining in 4 steps

1. 1. Learn the format. Download the official PDFs and highlight timing + instructions for your level.
2. 2. Run a baseline mock. Sit a full exam without pausing. Score yourself honestly to spot weak skills.
3. 3. Drill by skill. Rotate through Lesen, Hören, Schreiben, Sprechen drills using our practice hubs + official materials.
4. 4. Simulate weekly. Schedule full mock exams until you consistently exceed the pass mark (60%+).

## 💶 Costs, booking, and results

Exam fees vary by country and centre. Choose the provider that offers dates you can attend, is officially recognised by your institution, and delivers results in the timeframe you need. Popular centres fill quickly — book 6–8 weeks in advance when possible.

## ❓ FAQ

For general goals such as visas, work, or learning German, both certificates are accepted. Choose the test centre, schedule, and task types that feel most comfortable. For university entry, confirm whether TELC C1 Hochschule or Goethe C1 is on your faculty’s accepted list.

Both providers currently require you to sit the exam in person at an authorised centre. Some locations run “digital” formats on site (laptop-based Reading/Listening/Writing), but they remain supervised, in-centre exams.

Certificates themselves do not usually expire. However, employers, embassies, or universities may request a certificate issued within a certain time frame (often the last two years). Always follow the policy of the institution you are applying to.
