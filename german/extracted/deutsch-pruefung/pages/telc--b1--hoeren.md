### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The TELC B1 Hörverstehen (Listening) module takes 30 minutes and consists of three parts. You will hear various workplace and everyday texts.

#### Teil 1: Globalverstehen (Global Understanding)

• Listen to workplace conversations and situations

• Understand the main ideas and context

#### Teil 2: Detailverstehen (Detailed Understanding)

• Listen to longer workplace dialogues or announcements

• Identify specific details and information

#### Teil 3: Selektives Verstehen (Selective Understanding)

• Listen to professional conversations or presentations

• Extract specific information selectively
