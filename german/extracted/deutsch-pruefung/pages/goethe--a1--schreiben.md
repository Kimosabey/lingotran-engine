### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

This section tests your ability to schreiben (write) in German for everyday situations. The Schreiben (writing) section takes 15 minutes and has two distinct tasks.

#### Teil 1: Form-Filling Exercise

• Complete a form using given personal details and scenario prompts

• Transfer information accurately from text to form fields ✏️

#### Teil 2: Short Informal Message

• Write a brief informal/formal message (letter, note, or email) ✉️

• Use simple German sentences and basic vocabulary 📝
