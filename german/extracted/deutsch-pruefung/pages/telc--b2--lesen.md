### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The TELC B2 Leseverstehen (Reading) & Sprachbausteine module takes 90 minutes. It includes reading comprehension and grammar/vocabulary tasks.

#### Teil 1: Globalverstehen (Headlines)

• Match headlines to short texts

• Skim reading for main ideas

#### Teil 2: Detailverstehen (Detailed Reading)

• Read a longer text (article, report)

• Answer multiple choice questions

#### Teil 3: Selektives Verstehen (Ads)

• Match situational descriptions to advertisements

• Scan for specific information

#### Teil 4: Sprachbausteine Teil 1 (Grammar)

• Gap-fill text with multiple choice options

• Focus on grammar and structure

#### Teil 5: Sprachbausteine Teil 2 (Vocabulary)

• Gap-fill text using a word bank

• Focus on vocabulary and context
