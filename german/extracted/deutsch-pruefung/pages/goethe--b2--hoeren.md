### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The Goethe B2 Hören (Listening) module takes approximately 40 minutes. You hear multiple texts and complete tasks. Read the tasks first, then listen to the audio. Each task has only one correct answer. After listening, you have 5 minutes to transfer answers to the answer sheet. No aids (dictionaries, phones, mobile devices) are allowed.

#### Teil 1: Alltagsgespräche verstehen (Understanding Everyday Conversations)

• Listen to everyday conversations

• Task type: Wahl (2-gliedrig R/F) - True/False + Wahl (3-gliedrig MC) - Multiple Choice

#### Teil 2: Informationen verstehen (Understanding Information)

• Listen to informational audio (interviews, reports)

• Task type: Wahl (3-gliedrig MC) - Multiple Choice with 3 options

#### Teil 3: Aussagen verstehen (Understanding Statements)

• Listen to statements and match them to passages

• Task type: Zuordnung Passagen (Matching passages)

#### Teil 4: Vorträge verstehen (Understanding Lectures)

• Listen to a lecture or presentation
