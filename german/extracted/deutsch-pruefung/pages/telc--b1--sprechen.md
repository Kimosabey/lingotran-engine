### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The TELC B1 Mündlicher Ausdruck (Speaking) module takes approximately 15 minutes with a 20-minute preparation phase (Vorbereitungszeit). The exam can be conducted as a pair or individual examination (Paar- oder Einzelprüfung).

#### Teil 1: Einander kennenlernen (Getting to Know Each Other)

• Introduce yourself: name, residence, family, profession, hobbies

• Decide 'du' or 'Sie' based on the situation

#### Teil 2: Gespräch über ein Thema (Discussion on a Topic)

• Each partner receives a short text with a different opinion on the same topic

• Summarize your text, listen to your partner, then discuss

#### Teil 3: Gemeinsam etwas planen (Planning Together)

• Plan something together (party, trip, event)

• Make suggestions and react to partner's ideas
