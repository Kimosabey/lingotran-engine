### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The official telc Deutsch C1 Hörverstehen section takes about 40 minutes and has 48 points. It consists of Globalverstehen (global understanding), Detailverstehen (detailed understanding), and Informationstransfer (information transfer) tasks.

#### Teil 1: Globalverstehen (Global Understanding)

• Listen for the overall message, speaker stance, or central idea

• Solve 8 Zuordnungsaufgaben

#### Teil 2: Detailverstehen (Detailed Understanding)

• Listen to a longer spoken text such as a radio interview or discussion

• Choose the best answer from three options for each item

#### Teil 3: Informationstransfer (Information Transfer)

• Listen to one lecture, presentation, or seminar-style talk

• Complete missing information in a handout or notes
