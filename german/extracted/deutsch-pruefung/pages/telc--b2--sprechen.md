### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The TELC B2 Mündlicher Ausdruck (Speaking) module takes approximately 15 minutes with a 20-minute preparation phase (Vorbereitungszeit). The exam is conducted in pairs and consists of three parts testing your ability to speak about experiences, discuss topics, and plan activities together.

#### Teil 1: Über Erfahrungen sprechen (Talking About Experiences)

• Choose one topic from 7 options (book, film, trip, music event, sports event, important person, important experience)

• Speak for about 1½ minutes about your chosen topic

#### Teil 2: Diskussion (Discussion)

• Discuss a controversial topic with your partner

• State your opinion and defend it with arguments

#### Teil 3: Gemeinsam etwas planen (Planning Together)

• Plan an activity or event together with your partner

• Make suggestions and respond to your partner's ideas
