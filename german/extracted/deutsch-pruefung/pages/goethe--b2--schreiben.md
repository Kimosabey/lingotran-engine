### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The Goethe B2 Schreiben (Writing) module takes 75 minutes total. You write two texts. You can start with either task. Only texts on the answer sheet are graded. Write with a blue or black pen. No aids (dictionaries, phones, mobile devices) are allowed.

#### Teil 1: Produktion - Meinungsäußerung verfassen (Writing an Opinion)

• Write a forum post expressing your opinion (Forumsbeitrag)

• Free text: minimum 150 words

#### Teil 2: Interaktion - Persönliche Mitteilung verfassen (Writing a Personal Message)

• Write a personal message/correspondence (Nachricht)

• Free text: minimum 100 words
