## What the TELC B1 Exam Checks

Zertifikat Deutsch / telc Deutsch B1 tests everyday German for personal and professional communication at CEFR B1.

### Exam format and timing

The telc B1 qualification consists of a written examination and an oral examination.

#### Reading and language elements

90 minutes

Reading has three parts and language elements has two parts, with no break.

#### Listening

Approx. 30 minutes

Three listening parts in the written examination.

#### Writing

30 minutes

One writing task in the written examination.

#### Speaking

Approx. 15 minutes

Three speaking parts, pair examination, with 20 minutes of preparation time.

### Best for

#### Independent everyday German

Useful for personal and professional situations, including receptive and productive tasks.

#### Residence and citizenship preparation

Relevant when an authority asks for B1 German. Confirm the accepted certificate version before booking.

### How to use your practice time

- Receptive skills plus productive writing and speaking responses.
- Personal and professional topic vocabulary.
- Writing feedback for task completion, organization, and language accuracy.
