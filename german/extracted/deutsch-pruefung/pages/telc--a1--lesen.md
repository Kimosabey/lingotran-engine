### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

This section assesses your comprehension of short, everyday texts like notes, emails, and advertisements. This section consists of three parts.

#### Teil 1: Informal Text Comprehension

• Read several short, informal texts (emails or letters)

• Decide if given statements are true or false ✅ ❌

#### Teil 2: Situation-Based Text Selection

• Read different Advertisements/ Website descriptions 📝

• Choose the correct advertisement or text from options 'A' or 'B'

#### Teil 3: Signs & Notices Comprehension

• Read short information posters or notices

• Decide if statements about them are 'Richtig' or 'Falsch' ✅ ❌
