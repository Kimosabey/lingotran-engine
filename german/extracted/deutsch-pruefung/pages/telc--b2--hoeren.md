### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The TELC B2 Hörverstehen (Listening) module takes about 20 minutes. It consists of three parts with true/false questions.

#### Teil 1: Globalverstehen (Global Understanding)

• Listen to a news report or detailed information

• Decide if statements match the report

#### Teil 2: Detailverstehen (Detailed Understanding)

• Listen to a radio interview or conversation

• Understand specific details and opinions

#### Teil 3: Informationen verstehen (Understanding Information)

• Listen to short presentation or announcement

• Decide if statements are true or false
