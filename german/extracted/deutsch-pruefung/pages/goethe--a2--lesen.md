### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The Goethe A2 Reading module takes 30 minutes and tests your ability to understand everyday written texts. You will read various texts from daily life and complete four different tasks.

#### Teil 1: Understanding Media Texts

• Read short texts from newspapers, magazines, or online sources

• Understand information and simple arguments in everyday media

#### Teil 2: Information Boards & Event Programs

• Read information boards, event schedules, or public notices

• Find specific information quickly and understand practical details

#### Teil 3: Understanding Correspondence

• Read personal or semi-formal emails and letters

• Understand the writer's intention and key information

#### Teil 4: Understanding Advertisements

• Read various advertisements and announcements

• Match situations or needs to the appropriate advertisement
