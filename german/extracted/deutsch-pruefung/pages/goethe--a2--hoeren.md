### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The Goethe A2 Listening module takes 30 minutes and tests your ability to understand spoken German in everyday situations. You will hear various audio texts and complete four different tasks.

#### Teil 1: Radio, Answering Machine & Announcements

• Listen to information on the radio, answering machines, or public announcements

• Understand key details from everyday audio messages

#### Teil 2: Understanding Conversations

• Listen to a coherent conversation or dialogue

• Match statements or pictures to what you hear

#### Teil 3: Individual Conversations

• Listen to short individual conversations

• Answer questions about specific details

#### Teil 4: Radio Interview

• Listen to a radio interview or discussion

• Decide if statements are Richtig (true) or Falsch (false)
