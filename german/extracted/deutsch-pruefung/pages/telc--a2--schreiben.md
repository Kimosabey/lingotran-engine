### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The official telc Deutsch A2 Schreiben subtest is completed inside the shared 50-minute Lesen und Schreiben block. It has two tasks: one structured form task and one short personal letter task.

#### Teil 1: Formular ausfüllen (Complete a Form)

• Use the profile information to complete a registration form

• Write the five missing pieces of information

#### Teil 2: Brief schreiben (Write a Letter)

• Reply to a short everyday situation with a brief personal letter

• Choose 3 of the 4 given points and write 1-2 sentences for each
