### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The Goethe A2 Writing module takes 30 minutes and tests your ability to write in German for everyday situations. You will complete two different writing tasks.

#### Teil 1: Personal Message for Contact

• Write a personal message to maintain or establish contact

• Include relevant details and respond to a given situation

#### Teil 2: Semi-Official Message

• Write a semi-official message to regulate a situation

• Make requests, give information, or coordinate actions
