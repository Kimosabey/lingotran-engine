## What the TELC B2 Exam Checks

telc Deutsch B2 demonstrates upper-intermediate German in reading, listening, writing, speaking, and everyday communication.

### Exam format and timing

The telc B2 exam consists of a written and an oral examination and is available in digital, hybrid, and paper-based formats.

#### Reading and language elements

90 minutes

Reading has three parts and language elements has two parts, with no break.

#### Listening

Approx. 20 minutes

Three listening parts in the written examination.

#### Writing

30 minutes

One writing task in the written examination.

#### Speaking

Approx. 15 minutes

Three speaking parts, pair examination, with 20 minutes of preparation time.

### Best for

#### Advanced work and study preparation

Useful for more independent workplace language, complex input, and upper-intermediate communication.

#### University route research

Some routes may accept B2, but many German-taught programs require C1-level or university-specific proof.

### How to use your practice time

- Reading and listening tasks that require main ideas and specific details.
- Structured writing responses to given input.
- Paired speaking with examples, reasons, counterpoints, and follow-up answers.
