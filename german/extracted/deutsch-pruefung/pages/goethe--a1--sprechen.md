### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

This section tests your ability to communicate orally in German in everyday situations. The oral examination takes 15 minutes total and consists of three parts.

#### Teil 1: Self Introduction

• Introduce yourself using basic personal information 😊

• Share details about your name, age, and hobbies 🏠

#### Teil 2: Framing questions and answering

• Practice simple conversations with everyday topics 💭

• Ask and answer questions using picture cards 🖼️ - you will be given a theme and word

#### Teil 3: Framing requests

• Make simple requests based on topic in picture card

• Respond appropriately to requests from partner ✅
