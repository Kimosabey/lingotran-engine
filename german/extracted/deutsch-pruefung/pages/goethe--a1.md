## What the Goethe A1 Exam Checks

Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1 confirms basic German skills at CEFR A1 for adults.

### Exam format and timing

The exam has listening, reading, writing, and speaking sections. The speaking section is an oral group examination.

#### Listening

20 minutes

Short everyday conversations, telephone messages, and public announcements.

#### Reading

25 minutes

Short notes, advertisements, information signs, and notices.

#### Writing

Simple forms and a short personal text about an everyday situation.

#### Speaking

15 minutes

Self-introduction, everyday questions and answers, and simple requests.

### Best for

#### Basic German proof

Useful when you need to show beginner German for simple everyday communication.

#### Family reunification preparation

Relevant when a spouse visa route asks for A1-level German proof. Confirm accepted certificates with the German mission.

### How to use your practice time

- Personal information, family, shopping, work, school, and housing vocabulary.
- Forms, signs, short notes, slow listening, and simple public announcements.
- Speaking prompts for introductions, basic questions, answers, and requests.
