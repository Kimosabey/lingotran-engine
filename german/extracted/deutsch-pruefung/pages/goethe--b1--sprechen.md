### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The Sprechen (Speaking) module at B1 level takes approximately 15 minutes and includes three communicative tasks with your partner and the examiners.

#### Teil 1: Gemeinsam etwas planen (Planning Together)

• Plan an activity together with your partner (e.g., DVD evening, birthday party, trip)

• Discuss the given points and make suggestions (Vorschläge machen)

#### Teil 2: Ein Thema präsentieren (Present a Topic)

• Present a current topic using 5 slides (Folien)

• Slides: Introduction, Personal Experience, Home Country, Pros & Cons with Opinion, Conclusion

#### Teil 3: Über ein Thema sprechen (Discuss a Topic)

• Respond to questions from examiners and your partner about your presentation

• Give your partner feedback (Rückmeldung) on their presentation
