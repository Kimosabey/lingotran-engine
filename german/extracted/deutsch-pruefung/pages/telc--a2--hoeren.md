### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The official telc Deutsch A2 Hören subtest lasts about 20 minutes, has 15 items, and consists of three Teile. In the model test, you complete telephone notes, answer radio-information multiple-choice tasks, and match details from one longer conversation.

#### Teil 1: Ansagen am Telefon (Telephone Messages)

• You hear five telephone messages

• Complete five telephone notes with the missing information

#### Teil 2: Informationen aus dem Radio (Radio Information)

• You hear five short radio texts

• Choose the correct answer: a, b, or c

#### Teil 3: Ein Gespräch (Conversation)

• You hear one longer conversation

• Match the people or items to the correct option and write the letter
