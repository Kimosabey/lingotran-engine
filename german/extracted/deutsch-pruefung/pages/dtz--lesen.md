### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The DTZ Lesen (Reading) section takes 45 minutes and tests your ability to understand written German texts from everyday life. You will read catalogs, advertisements, newspaper articles, brochures, and letters.

#### Teil 1: Kataloge, Register, Verzeichnisse verstehen

• Read catalogs, registers, and directories

• Find specific information in structured texts

#### Teil 2: Allgemeine und spezifische Informationen in Anzeigen verstehen

• Read advertisements and notices

• Match situations to appropriate advertisements

#### Teil 3: Informationen und Meinungen in Pressetexten verstehen

• Read newspaper articles and formal communications

• Understand information, opinions, and arguments

#### Teil 4: Informationsbroschüren verstehen

• Read information brochures and guides

• Extract key information and understand instructions

#### Teil 5: Wörter in einem Brief ergänzen

• Complete gaps in a letter with appropriate words

• Understand text coherence and vocabulary in context
