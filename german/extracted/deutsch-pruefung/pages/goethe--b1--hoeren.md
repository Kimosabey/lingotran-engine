### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The Goethe B1 Listening module consists of four parts. You will hear several texts and complete tasks related to them.

#### Teil 1: Announcements and instructions

• Five short public announcements, platform calls, or recorded messages

• Audio is played twice 🔄

#### Teil 2: Understanding as an audience member

• One longer talk or announcement aimed at listeners in a public setting

• Audio is played once 🔄

#### Teil 3: Conversations between native speakers

• A dialogue between native speakers about everyday plans or events

#### Teil 4: Radio broadcasts and recordings

• A radio interview or feature with a moderator and guests
