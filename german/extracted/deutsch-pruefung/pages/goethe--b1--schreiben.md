### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The Goethe B1 writing module lasts 60 minutes in total and contains three tasks. Each Teil focuses on a different type of real-world interaction, mirroring the official exam structure.

#### Teil 1: Contact & Interaction

• Write a personal message that maintains or rebuilds contact

• Include a description, a reason, and a polite suggestion

#### Teil 2: Expressing an Opinion

• Share your personal opinion on a given topic

• Support your view with explanations, comparisons, or examples

#### Teil 3: Regulating a Situation

• React to an everyday situation that requires coordination

• Apologise, ask for something, or make a concrete request
