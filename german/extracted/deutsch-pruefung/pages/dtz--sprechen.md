### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The DTZ Sprechen (Speaking) section takes approximately 16 minutes and is conducted as a pair examination. You will introduce yourself, talk about experiences, and plan something together with your partner.

#### Teil 1: Über sich sprechen + Teil 1B: Auf Nachfragen reagieren

• Introduce yourself using keywords (Stichworten)

• Answer follow-up questions from the examiner

#### Teil 2: Über Erfahrungen sprechen + Teil 2B: Auf Nachfragen reagieren

• Talk about experiences using a picture prompt (Bildimpuls)

• Describe personal experiences related to the topic

#### Teil 3: Gemeinsam etwas planen

• Plan something together with your partner

• Discuss key points (Leitpunkten) and make suggestions
