### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The DTZ Hören (Listening) section takes 25 minutes and tests your ability to understand spoken German in everyday situations. You will hear telephone announcements, media information, conversations, and different opinions on topics.

#### Teil 1: Ansagen am Telefon, öffentliche Durchsagen verstehen

• Listen to telephone announcements and public messages

• Understand key information from everyday audio

#### Teil 2: Kurze Informationen in den Medien verstehen

• Listen to short information from media (radio, etc.)

• Identify main points and specific details

#### Teil 3: Alltägliche Gespräche verstehen

• Listen to everyday conversations between people

• Understand context, opinions, and details

#### Teil 4: Unterschiedliche Meinungen zu einem Thema verstehen

• Listen to different people expressing opinions

• Match statements to the correct speakers
