## What the Goethe A2 Exam Checks

Goethe-Zertifikat A2 confirms basic German skills at the second CEFR level for adults.

### Exam format and timing

The exam has listening, reading, writing, and speaking sections. The speaking section is an oral pair exam.

#### Listening

30 minutes

Everyday conversations, announcements, radio interviews, phone messages, and public announcements.

#### Reading

Short newspaper articles, emails, advertisements, and public information boards.

#### Writing

Messages related to your immediate living environment.

#### Speaking

15 minutes

Questions about yourself, talking about your life, and planning something with a partner.

### Best for

#### Everyday German checkpoint

Good for learners who can handle simple routines and want proof beyond beginner level.

#### Bridge to B1

A2 is usually a stepping stone toward B1 for residence, citizenship, or more independent work and study goals.

### How to use your practice time

- Routine exchanges about background, education, immediate needs, and familiar topics.
- Short texts where you find essential information quickly.
- Speaking roleplay for asking, answering, planning, agreeing, and clarifying.
