### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

This section tests your ability to understand spoken German through simple conversations and announcements. This section consists of three parts.

#### Teil 1: Audio-Picture Matching

• Match each audio to the corresponding picture or text 🖼️

• Audio is played twice 🔄

#### Teil 2: Short Announcements

• Listen to public announcements (Durchsagen) 🔊

• Audio is played only once - listen carefully! ⚠️

#### Teil 3: Voicemail Messages

• Listen to voicemail messages (Anrufbeantworter) 📞
