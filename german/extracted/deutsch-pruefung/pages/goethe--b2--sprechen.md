### About the Section

![Study bear mascot](https://deutsch-pruefung.de/_next/image?url=%2Fsection-assets%2Fbear-mascot.webp&w=384&q=75)

The Goethe B2 Sprechen (Speaking) module takes approximately 15 minutes. You have 15 minutes preparation time (for both pair and individual exams). You prepare alone and may take notes. In the exam, you should speak freely. No aids (dictionaries, phones, mobile devices) are allowed.

#### Teil 1: Produktion/Interaktion - Vortrag (Presentation)

• Give a prepared presentation on a chosen topic (Theme 1 or 2)

• Speak in front of an audience and answer follow-up questions

#### Teil 2: Interaktion - Diskussion (Discussion)

• Discuss a controversial topic with your partner

• Present and defend arguments pro and contra
